## Summary

- We don’t link bank accounts.
- Core cash data stays on your device by default.
- We only receive information you choose to send to us (like a waitlist email).

## Information We Collect

**Waitlist submissions.** If you join the waitlist, we collect your email address and any optional fields you submit.

**Support requests.** If you contact us, we collect the information you provide in your message.

**Basic website data.** Our website may log standard technical data such as IP address, device type, and browser. We use this for security and abuse prevention.

## Information We Do Not Collect

Kontana does not collect bank account credentials, transaction data from banks, or your on-device cash logs unless you explicitly export and share them.

## How We Use Information

- To respond to inquiries and support requests.
- To manage the waitlist and notify you about launch updates.
- To protect the site from spam, abuse, and fraud.

## Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with service providers who help us operate the website (for example, email delivery or security services), and only as needed to provide those services.

## Retention

We keep waitlist and support information as long as needed for the purposes described above, or until you request deletion.

## Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## Your Choices

- You can unsubscribe from emails using the link in our messages.
- You can request deletion of your waitlist or support information.

## Children’s Privacy

Kontana is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes

We may update this notice from time to time.

## Contact

If you have questions or requests, contact us at privacy@getkontana.com.
